Fun Facts about data mining




DATA MINING is a subfield of computer science.

Through definitions differ, they agree that data mining is the process of analyzing and discovering patterns in large data sets.

The overall goal of the data mining is to extract information from a data set and transform it into an understandable structure for further use.

This in turn allows various organizations like the government and large companies to collect, analyze and store data again, for futher use.
